Previously, I received my M.S. from SIST, Peking University, advised by Prof. Kunqing Xie and Prof. Guojie Song. I received my B.S. from Computer Science and Technology, Beijing Institue of Technology. After graduation, I spent two years(2023-2025) working at ByteDance and SRCB.

Research Interests

Now my research interest lies at developing trustworthy machine learning, LLm-based method for scientific-level applications. Besides, I also have interests in the following topics:

Graph Neural Networks: Enhancing the representative ability of Graph Neural Networks for specific scenarios, including dynamic social networks, spatial-temporal data mining and other chemical, commercial applications such as graph based recommender system, molecular graph.

Natural Language Process: Application of Language Model on structure data analysis, including document parsing and classification.

I am now open for academic collaborations and communications, please feel free to reach out to me.
